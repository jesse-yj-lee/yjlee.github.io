Yoonjae Lee

Hi, I am a PhD student in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin. My research focuses on optimization/game-based design of control and task allocation algorithms for multi-agent networks operating in adversarial/cooperative mission environments.

Prior to attending UT Austin, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science (BS) in Aerospace Engineering from University of California San Diego.
